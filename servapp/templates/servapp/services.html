Hospital Management System

is a web-based application that helps hospitals manage their daily activities in a simple and digital way. It stores patient information, doctor details, appointments, diagnosis, billing, and medicine records in one secure system. This reduces paperwork, saves time, and improves accuracy in hospital operations. Doctors and staff can easily access patient history, schedule appointments, and manage treatments, while patients receive faster and more organized services. Overall, the system makes hospital work more efficient, secure, and well organized, leading to better patient care.

Restaurant-Application

A popular Nepali fast food made by filling minced meat or vegetables inside small pieces of dough. They are usually steamed, but can also be fried or pan-fried, and are served hot with spicy sauce (achar). MOMOs are loved for their soft outer layer, juicy filling, and delicious taste, making them one of the most favorite snacks in Nepal and nearby regions.
